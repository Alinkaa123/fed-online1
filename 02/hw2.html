Stray Kids

or SKZ is a South Korean boy band formed by JYP Entertainment.


The band consists of eight members: Bang Chan, Lee Know, Changbin, Hyunjin, Han, Felix, Seungmin, and I.N. For undisclosed personal reasons, Woojin left the band in October 2019.

Name

Members conceptualized the name "Stray Kids" themselves, along with creating their own logo. The name originally referred to a lost child who wants to chase their dreams and evolved to represent the idea of finding a way out of the ordinary together

Korean studio albums

Go Live (2020), NoEasy(2021), 5-Star(2023), The Sound(2023), Dominate(2024), Giant(2024), Mixtape:Dominate(2025).
